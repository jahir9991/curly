Unitec. Think. Do

November 2015

Unitec

An interactive virtual reality experience that also tracks engagement levels by measuring people's brainwaves.

Idea

Prove that learning hands-on works by teleporting people into an immersive Unitec experience and then measuring their brainwaves.

Participants got 'teleported' into three immersive curriculums offered at Unitec versus a traditional lecture.

Execution

With Republik and Agent 86, we really got into the minds of prospective Unitec students.

Participants tried on a Samsung VR headset and got teleported into three immersive curriculums offered at Unitec versus a traditional lecture.

Then NeuroSky and Unity technology measured their brain engagement to see when their minds lit up most.

Whether they interacted with a massive NZ forest, an out-of-this-world music studio or a hyper-real vehicle workshop, the results were clear.Â 

In fact, we gave participants their results to see for themselves and share on Facebook. Brains were switching on all over the place. Everyone learned that hands-on learning engages the mind most, hands-down.
